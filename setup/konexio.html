Throughout the course of 2019 our Konexio team worked on a brand new matching platform that would match the volunteer mentors at Konexio to low income individuals who looked for specific skills.

We created a simple form for mentees to fill out and get matched to a mentor.

There is an account page where users can make sure their information is correct and that the server is running properly(the latter will be removed in hte official release).

A welcoming home page can direct users, whether mentor or mentee, to the right sign up or log in page.
